What You’re Probably Wondering.

Your team keeps their jobs. We’re not buying your business to strip it down. In most cases, day-to-day operations continue with the same people in the same roles.

Everything stays between us — whether we end up partnering or not. You can have a candid first conversation without worrying that your team or your customers will hear about it.

We look at your revenue, your customer relationships, your equipment and what the business has actually built over time.

It’s worth a conversation. We’ve partnered with shops that were thriving and shops that were struggling. If you’ve built something real over the years, that has value even if the last couple years were hard.

It moves at your pace and on your terms. Some deals come together in a few weeks. Others take a few months because the owner needs time to think or has specific timing around retirement or taxes.

Our core market is Long Island and the greater New York metro area, which is where our production facility and team are based. That said, we’re open to conversations with owners in nearby markets depending on the fit.
